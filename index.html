Art is everywhere, it fills each corner of Earth. I decided to capture the beauty of this planet as much as I can. To me, photography is a challenge that guides me through far lands, endless oceans, and distant mountains. As long as I have a camera in my hands, I'll try to capture the real feeling of adventure, amazingness, and emotion of the place I am. This is a journey.
Life is a Journey
phylosophy
This world hides unimaginable beauty, and we, as humans, must be prepared to catch those ephemeral moments. The forest outside the town where you grew up will not be the same in 10 years, the same goes for the beach you went to with your friends in that far summer. My job is to break that limit, and go beyond, creating a bridge between past and present, capturing all the faces this Earth provides us.
